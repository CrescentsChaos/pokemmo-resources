Heatran 6★

Requirements

- Sinnoh storyline (post-game) completed.
- OT caught national pokedex complete.
- 4 OT raid pokemon.

Location

Sinnoh: Stark Mountain

Mechanics

Ability: Flash Fire
Held Item: Leftovers
100%: Magmapool★
80%: Stealth Rock★
67%: Roar
50%: Iron Defense
40%: Adaptation
25%: Protective Shield

Moves

- Solarbeam
- Earth Power
- Magma Storm★
- Power Gem

Mechanic Details

Adaptation: Boss gains resistance to the most-hit type.

Weather: Magma Storm★ can remove weather or set Sun.

Magmapool★: Burns all Pokémon and deals 25% HP each turn.

Stealth Rock★: Works as normal but also activates at turn end.

Strategy

Use Fighting-types to bait Adaptation into Fighting. Bulky Ground/Water Pokémon should come in later. On the Roar turn, use Defog instead of Rapid Spin to clear hazards reliably.
